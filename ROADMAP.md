# Whence Roadmap

## Completed

- Task-to-calendar synchronization and visible version/update notices.
- Equipment summaries, case history, date-range export, and stable `device_id` grouping.
- Single-device service reports with completeness warnings, photos, and A4 print/PDF output.

## Next

- Customer monthly reports that preserve separate device sections under one customer.

## Later, only after real usage validates the need

- Recurring events and trash retention controls.
- Search/API caching if usage or quota requires it.

## Intentionally deferred

- ERP, inventory, quotations, multi-user permissions, graph/canvas, AI auto-organization, and full two-way conflict merging.
